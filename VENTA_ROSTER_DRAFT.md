# Venta Wild-Roster Draft тАФ who lives where? ЁЯЧ║я╕П

> **PROPOSED тАФ this is Lewis's homework B37, not a decision.** Every wild
> Fakeamon in the pool has been dealt a *draft* home area, matched to the
> six area vibes he invented (B8) and each creature's type, look, and blurb.
> **There are no wrong answers** тАФ to move someone, just tell Claude
> ("the crocodiles go to Snow Mountain!") or scribble on a printout. When
> Lewis signs off (or reshuffles), the picks get logged in `DECISIONS.md`
> and `tools/roster-200.json` is updated to match.
>
> Rules baked into the draft: **evolution lines stay together** (all stages
> share an area тАФ the basic stage is common, evolved stages rarer), levels
> follow B4 (early areas easy, far areas dangerous тАФ ranges below are
> Jeff-tunable `[TUNE]` drafts), and the three тЪая╕П OPMon-derived monsters
> are placed but stay out of the game until their license is confirmed.
> Wild-level ranges assume the badge order from B14: Gear тЖТ The Lagoon,
> Flame тЖТ The Factory, Wet тЖТ Snow Mountain.
>
> Names: the slugs below are Tuxemon working IDs. **Lewis invents every
> player-facing name** тАФ that's the rename pass, done area by area as each
> area gets built.
