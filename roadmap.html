🧭 The golden rules (how we build)

  1. One small step at a time. Finish a step, play it, then move on.
  2. Keep it runnable. The game should open and work after every step.
  3. Numbers live in obvious spots. HP, power, catch rate — easy to find and tweak.
  4. Commit after each working step, with a plain message.
  5. Stay in scope. If a step drags in a later milestone, stop and flag it.

🔎 Peer-review checkpoint: at each milestone boundary we get an outside/expert review of the next milestone's design & licensing — kept to our keep-it-simple, no-build, kid-friendly rules. The first one runs before M3 begins.
